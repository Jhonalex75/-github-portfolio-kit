# **App Name**: CyberEngineer Nexus

## Core Features:

- AI-Powered Design Review Tool: Automated analysis of CAD models and 2D engineering drawings to identify Design for Manufacturability (DFM) issues and ensure consistency against custom checklists and organizational standards.
- Generative Research Assistant Tool: AI-driven search across scientific literature, academic papers, and internal knowledge bases to synthesize comprehensive technical reports with accurate citations.
- Interactive 3D Simulation & IK: Real-time visualization and interactive manipulation of complex mechanical assemblies and robotic systems in a 3D environment, including Inverse Kinematics (IK) for simulating movement.
- AI-Moderated Communication Hub: A real-time chat platform with AI-driven semantic analysis to ensure all discussions remain professional, technically relevant, and adhere to engineering topics.
- Digital Thread Integration: Connect engineering standards (ASME, API) and technical requirements directly to project elements, parts, and RFIs, with seamless export to PLM/ERP systems.
- Unified Project Data & Asset Management: Centralized system for storing, indexing, and searching project-specific documents, historical engineering data, and digital assets, accessible through advanced search capabilities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background: A deep, immersive gradient transitioning from a dark indigo (#0A001F) to a rich violet (#2C005F), creating a celestial, infinite canvas for technical data.
- Primary/Interactive: Vibrant electric blue (#4080FF) for primary interactive elements, headings, and data highlights, providing a clear and energetic focal point.
- Accent/Glow: Luminous cyan (#00FFFF) used for subtle glows, active states, and critical alerts, evoking advanced holographic displays and reinforcing the cybernetic aesthetic.
- Headlines: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), bold and clean, ensuring high impact and a cutting-edge feel for titles and key information.
- Body text: 'Inter' (sans-serif), designed for optimal readability and a modern, professional appearance in longer content and data displays.
- Code/Monospace: 'Source Code Pro' (monospace), for clear and structured presentation of code snippets, logs, and technical specifications.
- Minimalist, geometric vector icons with optional glowing outlines or subtle fill transitions on interaction, representing data streams, network connections, and robotic functions in a streamlined, futuristic manner.
- Spacious, modular card-based layouts with clear content separation, designed to present complex technical information in an easily digestible and visually engaging format. Elements float against the deep gradient background, creating depth.
- Fluid and responsive transitions, including soft fades and subtle slides for navigation and content loading. Interactive elements feature reactive glows and micro-animations, providing intuitive feedback and enhancing the dynamic feel of the interface.
